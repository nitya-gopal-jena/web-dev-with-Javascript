Blinkit

Blinkit, formerly Grofers, is a quick-commerce platform based in Gurugram, India. Founded in 2013 by Albinder Dhindsa and Saurabh Kumar, it delivers groceries and essentials in minutes. Acquired by Zomato in 2022, Blinkit operates in over 30 cities, revolutionizing urban shopping with hyper-fast delivery and a tech-driven supply chain
